## Quality Standards (Maintained Across All Blocks)

### Structure for Each Use Case:
1. **Challenge** (50-60 words): Specific problem the company faced
2. **Approach** (60-70 words): Detailed solution implementation
3. **Definition** (50-60 words): Framework definition with key components
4. **Results** (60-70 words): Quantified outcomes with specific metrics
5. **Key Insight** (30-40 words): Strategic takeaway

### Total per use case: 250-300 words

### Quality Checklist:
- ✅ Real company examples with authentic scenarios
- ✅ Specific metrics and quantified results
- ✅ Strategic insights that generalize beyond the specific example
- ✅ Professional tone without marketing fluff
- ✅ Actionable frameworks that readers can apply
