### Phase 3: Content Generation Framework

#### Template Structure (Per Use Case)
```
Company: [Name]
Industry: [Sector]

Challenge: [50-75 words]
- Market context in [year]
- Specific problem faced
- Why it mattered
- The core challenge

Approach: [75-100 words]
- Initial strategy
- Key decisions made (2-3 specific)
- Timeline of evolution
- How they executed

Definition: [30-50 words]
"Their [Subcomponent]: [Specific example showing what great [subcomponent] looks like]"
- Must be quotable
- Must show the concept in action
- Must be specific and measurable

Results: [50-75 words]
- Valuation/market cap
- Growth metrics (users, revenue, market share)
- Industry impact
- Category creation/disruption

Key Insight: [25-50 words]
- Primary lesson applicable to users
- Specific, actionable takeaway
- Connects to subcomponent focus
```

#### Quality Checklist (Per Use Case)
- [ ] 200-300 words total
- [ ] Specific numbers and metrics throughout
- [ ] Definition section clearly shows the subcomponent
- [ ] Challenge explains context and stakes
- [ ] Approach shows specific decisions
- [ ] Results are quantified
- [ ] Key Insight is actionable
- [ ] Relevant to tech/SaaS audience
- [ ] No jargon without explanation
- [ ] Scannable with clear sections

### Phase 4: Batch Generation Strategy

#### Option A: Block-by-Block (Recommended)
**Process**:
1. Generate all 6 subcomponents for one block (36 use cases)
2. Review and refine for quality
3. Deploy and test
4. Gather feedback
5. Move to next block

**Timeline**: 15 blocks × 2 days = 30 days
**Pros**: Manageable chunks, quality control, iterative feedback
**Cons**: Longer total timeline

#### Option B: Parallel Generation
**Process**:
1. Generate all 540 use cases using AI assistance
2. Human review and refinement in batches
3. Deploy all at once
4. Comprehensive testing

**Timeline**: 5-7 days intensive work
**Pros**: Faster completion, consistent quality
**Cons**: Harder to maintain quality, less feedback

#### Option C: Hybrid Approach (Recommended)
**Process**:
1. **Week 1**: Generate Blocks 2-4 (108 use cases) - Customer & Product focus
2. **Week 2**: Generate Blocks 5-8 (144 use cases) - GTM & Growth focus
3. **Week 3**: Generate Blocks 9-12 (144 use cases) - Execution & Teams focus
4. **Week 4**: Generate Blocks 13-16 (144 use cases) - Scale & Expansion focus
5. **Week 5**: Review, refine, deploy all

**Timeline**: 4-5 weeks
**Pros**: Balanced speed and quality, thematic grouping
**Cons**: Still significant effort
