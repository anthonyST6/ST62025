What is Timeline Gantt or Roadmap?

A visual timeline showing the build and launch phases of your prototype, including dependencies, milestones, and resource allocation.

Why It Matters

Prototypes often drift without clear timelines. A visual roadmap keeps the team aligned, stakeholders informed, and momentum maintained.

How to Implement

Roadmap Elements:

  • Phases: Design, Build, Test, Launch
  • Milestones: Key deliverables and decisions
  • Dependencies: Blocking relationships
  • Resources: Team allocation
  • Buffer Time: Risk mitigation

Creation Process:

  1. List all major tasks
  2. Estimate durations
  3. Identify dependencies
  4. Assign resources
  5. Add buffer for unknowns
  6. Review with stakeholders

Examples

  • 6-week prototype: 2 weeks design, 3 weeks build, 1 week test
  • Key milestone: Week 4 - First user test
  • Launch date: March 15 with 5 pilot customers

Key Metrics

  • On-time delivery rate
  • Milestone completion
  • Schedule variance
  • Resource utilization
